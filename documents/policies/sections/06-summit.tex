\section{Summit on Development of Engineering
  Societies}\label{summit-on-development-of-engineering-societies}

\subsection{Attendance}\label{attendance-3}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The attending members of the CFES Team shall be:

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    All members of the Board
   \item
    1 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Academic Portfolio
   \item
    1 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Services Portfolio
  \end{enumerate}

 \item
  Exceptions can be made by the Board to allow additional Commissioners to attend

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    The respective Vice President overseeing the Commissioner must provide a letter to the Board detailing the tasks the individual will complete at SDES and why they must attend.
   \item
    These letters will be discussed and voted on at the Board meeting after the submission of the letter and require a majority vote to approve the individual's attendance.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Addition General Assembly}\label{addition-general-assembly}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  During Summit on Development of Engineering Societies, there must be a meeting of the General Assembly, which is held in addition to the Annual General Meeting at CELC.
 \item
  At a minimum, the following content must be included on the General Assembly agenda:

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    Budget update \& ratification of current finances
   \item
    Motions presented by the National Executive
   \item
    Motions presented by the members

  \end{enumerate}

 \item
  In creating the agenda for this General Assembly session, the following guidelines must be adhered to:
  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    A preliminary agenda must be distributed a minimum of 14 days in advance of the Annual General Meeting in both French and English
   \item
    Motions originating from the Board, Board Members, or National Executive must be submitted for the preliminary agenda, with reference documents included, or will otherwise be subject to a two-thirds (2/3) majority threshold to be added to the agenda
  \end{enumerate}

 \item
  All PowerPoint presentations prepared for SDES must be translated into both languages and the presenter's language should be distributed to the delegates.

\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Content}\label{content-1}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The following content should be included in SDES annually, in addition to the General Assembly session outlined above:

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    CFES introductory sessions
   \item
    Any relevant or mandated reports, including action items from transition reports
   \item
    Board updates, work plans \& objectives
   \item
    CELC session brainstorming
   \item
    Closed accountability session
  \end{enumerate}

\end{enumerate}
