\section{Information Handling and Security}\label{information-handling-and-security}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Personal information is collected by the CFES from time to time, this information shall be kept private and secure.
 \item
  Personal information is defined by the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act as:

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    Information relating to the race, national or ethnic origin, colour, religion, age, sex, sexual orientation or marital or family status of the individual,
   \item
    Information relating to the education or the medical, psychiatric, psychological, criminal or employment history of the individual or information relating to financial transactions in which the individual has been involved,
   \item
    Any identifying number, symbol or other particular assigned to the individual,
   \item
    The address, telephone number, fingerprints or blood type of the individual,
   \item
    The personal opinions or views of the individual except where they relate to another individual,
   \item
    Correspondence sent to an institution by the individual that is implicitly or explicitly of a private or confidential nature, and replies to that correspondence that would reveal the contents of the original correspondence,
   \item
    The views or opinions of another individual about the individual, and
   \item
    The individual's name where it appears with other personal information relating to the individual or where the disclosure of the name would reveal other personal information about the individual.
  \end{enumerate}

 \item
  The CFES defines personal information in the same way.
 \item
  No official of the CFES shall ask for personal information with the following exceptions:

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    First and last Name
   \item
    Email address
   \item
    Phone number
   \item
    Education status. This includes the program, year, place of education, international, region of secondary graduation, student status, and courses taken.
   \item
    Date of birth
   \item
    Personal pronouns
   \item
    Preferred language
   \item
    Special needs
   \item
    Gender identification
   \item
    Age
   \item
    Views on the state of engineering education at their school
   \item
    Disclosure of personal experiences relating to their educational studies
   \item
    Ethnicity
   \item
    Nationality
  \end{enumerate}

 \item
  If more information is needed for a specific case, the President may grant special permission for the information to be collected.
 \item
  In all cases, the purpose for the collection of personal information and the individuals who are able to access the information (with the exception of cloud storage administrators) must be explicitly stated on the collection form or at the time of collection.
 \item
  Documents containing personal information are considered classified documents and the storage and transfer of personal information is subject to the Information Technology Security policy.
\end{enumerate}
