\section{Canadian Engineering Leadership Conference
  (CELC)}\label{canadian-engineering-leadership-conference-celc}

\subsection{Attendance}\label{attendance-2}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The attending members of the CFES Team shall be:
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  All members of the Board
 \item
  1 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Academic Portfolio
 \item
  2 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Communications Portfolio
 \item
  1 commissioner{[}s{]} from the External Portfolio
 \item
  1 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Finance Portfolio.
 \item
  2 commissioner{[}s{]} from the Services Portfolio

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    Exceptions can be made by the Board to allow additional Commissioners to attend

    \begin{enumerate}
     \item
      The respective Vice President overseeing the Commissioner must provide a letter to the Board detailing the tasks the individual will complete at CELC and why they must attend.
     \item
      These letters will be discussed and voted on at the Board meeting after the submission of the letter and require a majority vote to approve the individual's attendance.
    \end{enumerate}
  \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Annual General Meeting}\label{annual-general-meeting}

\subsubsection{Structure}\label{structure}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The Annual General Meeting (AGM) of the Federation takes place at CELC in January each year.
 \item
  This General Assembly consists of at least two sessions, with at least one day between the sessions for counter-proposal writing.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Session One}\label{session-one}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Generally, Session One of the AGM consists of:
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Budget update for current year
 \item
  Budget presentation \& approval for incoming year
 \item
  Updates from the Board
 \item
  Applications for Membership
 \item
  Motions presented by the National Executive
 \item
  Motions presented by the members

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    Any motions for which there are no intentions to counter-propose may be voted on during Session One.
   \item
    Should a member declare an intention to counter-propose, the motion is tabled to Session Two.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Session Two}\label{session-two}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Generally, Session Two of the AGM consists of:
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Presentation \& debate of counterproposals, as well as any additional proposals not put forward for Session One
 \item
  Election and ratification of President, VP Services, VP Finance \& Administration, VP Academic, VP Communications, and National Councillors
 \item
  Ratification of Regional Ambassadors
 \item
  Election and ratification of host members for Activities
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{General Assembly}\label{general-assembly}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The original language of all resolutions should be noted
 \item
  A preliminary agenda must be distributed a minimum of 14 days in advance of the Annual General Meeting in both French and English
 \item
  Motions originating from the Board, Board Members, or National Executive must be submitted for the preliminary agenda, with reference documents included, or will otherwise be subject to a two-thirds (2/3) majority threshold to be added to the AGM agenda
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Content}\label{content}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The organizing committee shall work with the National Executive to develop the schedule for CELC.
 \item
  In addition to the General Assembly, the following should be included:
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Accountability Session
 \item
  Student-run sessions
 \item
  Full or Standing Student Partner session(s)
 \item
  Sponsor sessions
 \item
  Motion Writing session(s)
 \item
  Session to discuss all Board positions and suggest major goals for incoming National Executive
 \item
  Additional time to support the Activity bidding processes, as well as National Executive and National Councillor election processes, including campaign speeches

  \begin{enumerate}
   \item
    All PowerPoint presentations prepared for CELC must be translated into both languages, and the presenter's language of the session must be provided to the delegates.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}
