\section{Amendments}\label{amendments}

\subsection{Substantive Amendments}\label{substantive-amendments}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The Policy Manual of the Federation may be repealed or amended by resolution of the General Assembly, or by two-thirds (2/3) majority vote of the Board.
 \item
  Any amendments passed by the Board shall be communicated to all members within ten (10) business days.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Non-Substantive
 Amendments}\label{non-substantive-amendments}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Amendments to the Policy Manual that are simply corrective measures to ensure accurate translation and grammatical structure may be made by the Board Chair, provided these amendments do not change the concept or spirit of the point being amended, and that the Board is given notice of any such changes.
\end{enumerate}
