\section{Advocacy}\label{advocacy}

\subsection{Membership Input}\label{membership-input}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  For any external meetings attended by the National Executive, a meeting package consisting of any presentations the National Executive is planning on giving is to be sent out to the Board a minimum of 7 days before the start of the meeting for input, or as soon as it is made available.
 \item
  Once this is done, Regional Ambassadors may consult the membership on the subject of these presentations at their discretion.
 \item
  Feedback for the respective presentation will be gathered via a form made by the National Executive and completed by the Regional Ambassador.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Official statements, Memorandums, Letters of support, and
 Communique}\label{official-statements-memorandums-letters-of-support-and-communique}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Any official statement made by the federation must be approved by the Board according to the following process.
 \item
  Examples of reasons for public statements include
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Membership request for local advocacy
 \item
  Request of partners
 \item
  World events
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Statement in line with
 stances}\label{statement-in-line-with-stances}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The statement shall be prepared with an accompanying document that demonstrates how it relates to the CFES document of stances
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  Regional Ambassadors are to distribute both documents for feedback with a minimum feedback period of 48 hours
 \item
  The Board may then hold a vote to approve the statement, at which point it can be disseminated
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Statements not explicitly in line with
 stances}\label{statements-not-explicitly-in-line-with-stances}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item
  The statement shall be prepared with an accompanying document that explains the motivation for the statement.
 \item
  Regional Ambassadors are to distribute both documents for feedback with a minimum feedback period of 14 days
 \item
  At their discretion Regional Ambassadors may request modifications to the statement based of feedback
 \item
  The modified statement is to be distributed along with a response to the feedback.
 \item
  There is a period for feedback on modified statements of 7 days
 \item
  The Board may then hold a vote to approve the statement, at which point it can be publicly disseminated
\end{enumerate}
