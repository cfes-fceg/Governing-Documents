\section{Article 4 - Membership}
\label{article-4---membership}

\subsection{Regular Member (Engineering Society)}
\label{regular-member-engineering-society}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item A regular member of the Federation is a society representing undergraduate engineering students at a Canadian higher education institution having an engineering program accredited, or in the process of accreditation, by the Canadian Engineering Accreditation Board, that represents the students' concerns to their faculty and higher education institution administration and which received the support of the General Assembly as specified in Article 4.3.2.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Cooperative Regular Member}
\label{cooperative-regular-member}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item Multiple societies may apply to co-represent the undergraduate engineering students at their institution as if eligible to be an ordinary Regular Member as defined in Article 4.1 where there is no single society fitting the description.
 \item A Cooperative Regular Member is identical to an ordinary Regular Member in all ways except the voting representative must be confirmed by each cooperative society President.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Application for Membership}
\label{application-for-membership}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item To become a regular member an organization must submit a written application to the Vice-President Finance and Administration, who must immediately inform the members of such an application.
 \item The members' assembly must then make a decision regarding the organization admission, and this no later than the annual meeting following the organisation's submission of the application.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Observer Status (Engineering Society)}
\label{observer-status-engineering-society}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item An observer of the Federation is a society representing undergraduate engineering students at a Canadian higher education institution having an engineering program accredited, or in the process of accreditation, by the Canadian Engineering Accreditation Board, that represents the students' concerns to their faculty and higher education institution administration, and which received the support of the Board as specified in the Bylaws.
 \item Observer status is valid for one year after an application is approved after which point the society in question must choose to apply to become a regular member as per Article 4.1.1, apply for a one-time extension or withdraw membership both as per the Bylaws.
\end{enumerate}
