\section{Article 2 - Definitions}
\label{article-2---definitions}

Board Members: Members of the Board, as specified in Article 10.

Members: Member societies, as specified in Article 4.

National Executive: President and Vice-Presidents as specified in Article 6.

Resolution: Motion presented as ``Proposal'' and approved by a majority vote.

Special Resolution: Motion presented as ``Special Proposal'' and approved by a supermajority vote.

Signing Officer: Person who has the right to authorize financial transactions on behalf of the CFES, as specified in Article 13.

General Assembly: Meeting of the Members.
