\section{Article 12 - Activities and Services}
\label{article-12---activities-and-services}

\subsection{Overview}
\label{overview}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item The activities of the Federation are the Canadian Engineering Competition (CEC), the CFES Canadian Engineering Leadership Conference (CELC), the CFES Conference on Diversity in Engineering (CDE), the CFES Conference on Sustainability in Engineering (CSE) and the CFES Summit on Development of Engineering Societies (SDES).
 \item The specifications of responsibilities between the CFES and the activities are outlined in the CFES Activity Partnership Agreements.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Organization of Activities and Services}
\label{organization-of-activities-and-services}

\subsubsection{Organizing Members}
\label{organizing-members}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item Only active members, as outlined in Article 4.1 of this constitution, and the Board, can organize the activities or services of the Federation.
 \item Organizing Members are appointed in General Assembly.
\end{enumerate}

\subsubsection{Activity Managers}
\label{activity-managers}

\begin{enumerate}
 \item The Activity Managers are appointed by the active member appointed in General Assembly.
 \item Their term of office spans from the time of appointment until the resolution of any accounts receivable or payable at the conclusion of the activity in question.
 \item They have the exclusive responsibility of all that is related to their activity, including, but without limitation to, the financial accountability and their audition by an external auditor, the appointment of an organising committee, the signature of official documents, etc.
 \item They answer directly to their Advisory Board, the organizing member and to the Board.
\end{enumerate}
